Hi, I'm so glad you're here!

My passion for hairstyling began when I was just 14 years old. I would spend hours watching YouTube tutorials and practicing late into the night, determined to recreate every look I fell in love with. What started as a hobby quickly became my purpose.

I began styling hair for my sister and her friends for special events like proms, homecomings, and graduations—and that's when I knew this was what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.

After high school, I pursued my dream professionally by attending Paul Mitchell Schools, where I earned my cosmetology license. From there, I launched my freelance career and have continued to grow, refine my craft, and fall deeper in love with hairstyling every day.

To me, this isn't just a job,it's something I truly enjoy. It never feels like work.

I'm passionate about helping women feel confident, beautiful, and like the best version of themselves, especially on the most important days of their lives.

BEAUTY TIPS

Volume Tip

To achieve beautiful volume, teasing is key—but even more important is over-directing the hair. This creates lift and structure that lasts all day.

Polished Look Tip

A flawless hairstyle always starts with proper prep. Without a strong foundation, it's hard to achieve that smooth, polished finish.

Frizz Control Tip

When using hairspray, spray in the direction you want the hair to stay. Spraying randomly can cause frizz and unwanted flyaways.
