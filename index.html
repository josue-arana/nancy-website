BEAUTY TIPS

Volume Tip

To achieve beautiful volume, teasing is key—but even more important is over-directing the hair. This creates lift and structure that lasts all day.

Polished Look Tip

A flawless hairstyle always starts with proper prep. Without a strong foundation, it's hard to achieve that smooth, polished finish.

Frizz Control Tip

When using hairspray, spray in the direction you want the hair to stay. Spraying randomly can cause frizz and unwanted flyaways.
